Founded in 2010, RapidSend Srvcs began with a clear goal—to simplify gift wrapping, packaging, and shipping for small businesses. Starting with partnerships on eBay and Amazon, we quickly grew into a trusted logistics and packaging partner, now proudly delivering through Amazon Hub. What started as a small operation has grown into a full-scale service center helping brands across industries.

What We Do

We offer end-to-end solutions in gift wrapping, packaging, and shipping. From custom packaging to transportation and last-mile delivery, our services are tailored for eCommerce businesses looking to scale efficiently. Whether you’re shipping personalized gifts or high-volume inventory, we make sure every item is packed with care and delivered with speed.

Why Businesses Choose Us

At RapidSend Srvcs, we eliminate your supply chain hassles. Our business-to-business model offers fast turnaround times, cost savings, and consistent quality in presentation. With a wide range of logistics options and a hands-on team, we help brands enhance customer satisfaction and boost sales—one shipment at a time.
